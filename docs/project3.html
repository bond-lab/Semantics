HG8011: Project 3 (Individual: 15%)

The third project for HG8011: Detecting Meaning with Sherlock Holmes. This project is divided into two parts: sentiment and definitions.

Sentiment (10%)
  • If you have not yet done so, read the whole story: The Red-Headed League (REDH) (another version)
  • Tag sentiment for all words that need to be tagged in the sentences assigned to you.
    • Most words (85–90%), will be neutral, but some words convey positive or negative sentiment
    • More detailed instructions are given in the » Simple Sentiment Guide «
    • The tool works best under Chrome or Firefox.
    • You will be assessed on
      • Completion: is everything that shows sentiment tagged?
      • Recall: have you only tagged things that show sentiment?
      • Accuracy: are the tags reasonable? (do they agree more than a threshold with an expert annotation)
  • Estimated time 2-5 hours (but it may take longer). Don't overthink things.
  • You should have checked every word by the due date.
  • Comment words where you are not sure of the sentiment
  • You can change sense tags, but are not encouraged to.
  • Due date: 2016-04-01 23:59 (Fri)
Definitions (5%)
  • You are required to add new definitions and examples to 5 concepts. The goal is to write a better definition (clearer, easier to understand, more accurate) for the same concept.
  • Use the tool provided, add as follows:
    a definition; another definition if needed --- an example; another example if needed
    That is: use semicolons to separate sentences and three hyphens to separate examples from definitions
    e.g. lexicographer
  • The definitions should be easy to understand in isolation
  • They should distinguish the sense from other senses
  • Follow the Wiktionary first principles
  • You will be assessed on
    • Completion: have you defined all five concepts and given examples
    • Suitability: are the definitions reasonable (do they give a good idea of the concept's meaning)
    • Circularity: have you defined the word using the word (or another form of) itself (not a good thing)
    • Originality: are your definitions new (and thus legal)
  • Due date: 2016-04-01 23:59 (Fri)
